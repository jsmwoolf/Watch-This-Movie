FAQ

1. What is this site about?
Want to know what movie to watch? This site is designed for anonymous or signed up users to upvote and downvote movie selections to help you decide.
2. Do I have to have an account to use all features?
You do not have to be a user to vote on any movie selections, but you must sign up if you would like to suggest a movie and see how good (or bad) everyone feels about it.
3. Do I have to pay anything?
There is no charge.
